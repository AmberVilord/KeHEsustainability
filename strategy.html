Strategy Overview

A Systems Approach to Sustainable Distribution

No single change can transform a national logistics network. Real progress comes from coordinated improvements across fleet efficiency, route design, regional infrastructure, and community partnerships.

Our sustainability strategy focuses on three interconnected pillars:

Pillar 1 — Fleet Efficiency

Improving how trucks operate to reduce fuel consumption and emissions.

Pillar 2 — Network Optimization

Rethinking how shipments move through regional hubs, relay points, and transportation modes.

Pillar 3 — Community Integration

Using existing delivery routes to support food recovery, supplier access, and workforce development.
