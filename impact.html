DPaaS Add-On Impact (Napkin Estimate)

If approximately 30% of long-haul miles can platoon and trailing savings average about 6%, total fleet fuel reduction from the DPaaS layer is roughly 1.5–2%.

While percentage gains appear modest, this can still represent hundreds of thousands of gallons for large fleets with minimal new physical infrastructure.
