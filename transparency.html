Why This Fits KeHE

Aligned with KeHE's Operating Model and Mission

KeHE combines national distribution capability with a mission-driven approach to wellness, sustainability, and community partnership.

Idle-to-Impact aligns with this philosophy because it:

  • improves operational efficiency instead of replacing infrastructure
  • reduces environmental impact using proven logistics practices
  • supports measurable sustainability reporting for B-Corp certification
  • strengthens community distribution capacity without increasing cost

Most importantly, this strategy builds on KeHE's existing strengths rather than requiring an entirely new system.
