How We Estimate Environmental Impact

All impact projections presented on this site are illustrative estimates designed to demonstrate how operational improvements may influence fuel consumption and emissions across a large delivery network.

These estimates reference publicly available transportation benchmarks including:

  • diesel emissions factors
  • fleet fuel efficiency studies
  • idle consumption data
  • verified aerodynamic performance ranges
  • rail freight efficiency comparisons

Actual results will vary depending on operating conditions and fleet configuration.
